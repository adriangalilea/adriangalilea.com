## Constraints

Carmack, Ango, and Peterson touch the same truth from different angles:

- **Carmack** (pragmatic): Restrict flexibility → do something better
- **Ango** (philosophical): Consistent constraints → style emerges
- **Peterson** (psychological): Without rules → slave to passions
