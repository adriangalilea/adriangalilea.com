Inspired by [this product](https://www.nedrelow.com/products/magic-sleeve?variant=31842686926913). When I saw it I knew I wanted it, but also wanted to learn how to make one my way. I also wanted to learn how to sew, so it all aligned.

## August 2020

Bought [natural wool felt sheets](https://www.loralora.com/products/fieltro-jaspeado-de-lana-100-grande?_pos=2&_psq=fieltro-lana&_ss=e&_v=1.0) and [neodymium magnets](https://www.amazon.es/Neodimio-Unidad-Extrem-Fuerte-magenesis%C2%AE/dp/B06X977K8L), had to try a few until I settled on the desired size/strength.

Borrowed my aunt's sewing machine and knowledge. Thanks!

The first version was just a prototype, but I found myself loving to use it. I used some boring color for that one and saved the cool wool for v2.

Observations: Loved to use it more than expected. Lacked consistency.

## September 2020

Biggest problem was consistency. Considered adding thicker wool, but it didn't feel enough so I went to my local fabric store with v1 and asked for possible solutions. She suggested a fabric typically used on costumes, also used on shirt collars. Bought it.

Aesthetics: Yellow + dark gray combo. The stitching matches the opposite color on each side — yellow side with gray stitching, gray side with yellow stitching.
