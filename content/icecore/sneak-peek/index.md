IceCore creates unforgeable proofs of existence for any digital document using the Bitcoin blockchain. No accounts. No fees. No trust required.

Hash your file, submit to the OpenTimestamps network, get anchored in Bitcoin. Your data stays private; only the hash gets timestamped. The proof is a self-contained `.icecore` bundle you can verify independently forever, with or without us.
